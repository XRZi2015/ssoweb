排序

    <#assign arrays=[3,2,5,7,6,1]/>
  • 未排序
    • <#list arrays as array> ${array},
  • Controller排序
    • <#list sort_int(arrays) as array> ${array},
  • Freemarker排序
    • <#list arrays?sort as array> ${array},
  • 获取下标
    • <#list arrays as array> ${array_index},
  • 获取长度
    • ${arrays?size}
  • 通过下标取值
    • ${arrays[1]}
